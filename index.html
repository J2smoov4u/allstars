Meet the Team

FC Scwalbe

The FC Schwalbe v. 1899 eV is one of the largest sports clubs in Hanover with more than 1,000 members. In the south of the state capital, the members can use a wide range of sports, including gymnastics, volleyball and tennis. In his hometown part of Döhren, FC Schwalbe is the largest sports club. The association, founded in 1899 in Döhren, maintains a long tradition.

Mittelfeld Allstars

Here at Mittelfeld Allstars we aim to give back to our community. We do this on the court by showing our love for the game & by giving 100%. Off-court we strive to be good role-models and commit to various acts to enhance our environment.
